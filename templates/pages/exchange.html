FAQs

Do you provide assistance with work permit and travel documentation?

Yes. While AIESEC does not issue or process work permits or visas, we support you throughout your exchange by helping you understand the requirements for the country you are going to.

Is there an age limit for this program?

As an international youth-led organization, our mission is to empower and create opportunities for individuals between the ages of 18-30 years old.

Are there any academic or experience requirements for the exchange opportunities?

Requirements vary based on each opportunity. AIESEC itself has no specific requirements to sign up; we do our best to help find projects that meet your experience level.

What is the selection process like?

Once you apply, the project hosts will contact you for an interview. If successful, you will receive an acceptance note to move forward with the program.

What countries can I go to?

AIESEC is present in over 100 countries. Focus countries include Spain, Germany, Hong Kong, Mexico, The Netherlands, and Italy.
