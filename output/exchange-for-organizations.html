How it Works

1

Contact Us

Meet with one of our exchange representatives to guide your team on the AIESEC exchange process.

Time: 1 week
2

Sign Contract

Open one or more opportunities within your company to be posted on our international job posting platform.

Time: 2 weeks
3

Hire Talent

AIESEC will provide you with a shortlist of candidates to choose from for each position after 2 weeks and the interview process begins.

Time: 2–4 weeks
4

Processing & Preparation

Once selected, AIESEC will support the exchange participant through their work permit application process and our logistics debrief.

Time: 5–8 weeks (after submitting application to IEC)
5

Project Begins

After confirming project timeline and dates, your exchange participant has arrived and is ready to start their first day on the job!

Time: 13–78 weeks
