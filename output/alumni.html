once an AIESECer
always an AIESECer

We hope you didn't forget, but hey there fellow AIESECer, it's been a while! Don't worry, there are still plenty of ways you can continue to help us develop and deliver the best leadership experiences to young people across Canada and around the world. Just take a look below to see how you can continue building life-lasting connections and impact.

Get Involved

Pass on the Leadership

The best way for the next generation to learn and grow, is to take a look into the past, and what better way than for them to learn from our alumni! Whether you want to mentor an AIESECer or join a Board of Advisors, there are plenty of opportunities to share the knowledge and help empower more future leaders!

Let's share the knowledge!

Be our Guest

As we start to host more and more events, the entity is always on the lookout for more alumni to join us at our events and workshops to shed more light on leadership and the impact AIESEC can make on everyday life. Of course, who better to speak on leadership than the experts who have been through all kinds of experiences (that's you ;))

I want to be a guest speaker!

Hire International Talent

At the heart of everything we do, there is one thing we aim to do best.. exchange of course. Think your company could use some fresh skill from all around the world with expedited VISA processing? You know exactly who to go to!

Support our Programs
