Youth to Business

Here in AIESEC, we aim to empower every young person in Canada and around the world to achieve their leadership potential. In order to accomplish this, one of our missions is to bridge the gap between young people and businesses so that they can learn from each other, grow together, and create lasting positive changes on our communities and industries.

We do this with our Youth to Business series.

Y2B Forum

Connect with hundreds of students and youth across Canada for the largest youth-led leadership event of the year!

Tackle and dive deep into important topics surrounding leadership and the future of work at our theme panels.
Build meaningful brand connections and promote your business to new audiences.

Y2B Summit

Meet the leading bodies of AIESEC in Canada and join us for one of our seasonal summits to help ignite the leadership across Canada!

Connect with the Local Presidents of AIESEC in Canada whom are each running their own chapters across Canada.
Gain feedback and insight into industry topics from our value-driven leadership.
Network with promising young leaders entering the workforce.
Y2B Survey

Gain insight on the youth perspective on different industries, employer practices, and a variety of other relevant topics to employment and business.

Learn more about the 18-29 range market and their key market traits.
Gain valuable insight and data on the youth market to strengthen your reach and target key audiences.
Discover turnkey solutions to your business operations through fresh new insight.
