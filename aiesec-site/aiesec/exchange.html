How it works

1

Click the button below to signup and be contacted by a local AIESEC representative within 2–3 business days.

Everyone's leadership journey looks different. Our representatives will meet with you to understand your wants and needs and help you find the perfect opportunity!

FAQs

Do you provide assistance with work permit and travel documentation?
Yes. While AIESEC does not issue or process work permits or visas, we support you throughout your exchange by helping you understand the requirements for the country you are going to. Every destination has its own rules, and we work closely with our AIESEC partners abroad to guide you on what documents you need.
Is there an age limit for this program?
As we are an international youth-led organization, our mission is to empower and create opportunities for individuals between the ages of 18–30 years old, so all volunteers and interns must be within this range.
Are there any academic or experience requirements?
As our projects are all over the world and partnered with many different organizations, the requirements for projects vary based on each opportunity. AIESEC itself has no specific requirements to signup so we do our best to help find projects that meet your own experience level and abilities.
What countries can I go to?
AIESEC is present in over 100 countries, but to ensure we deliver the highest quality experiences, AIESEC in Canada aims to partner with expert international teams. Some of our current focus countries are: Spain, Germany, Hong Kong, Mexico, The Netherlands, Italy and several other partners that are always getting refreshed each year!
