FAQs

Are these paid positions?
AIESEC is a non-for-profit international organization that relies on the support and commitment of all our amazing volunteers. That being said, there are many opportunities in AIESEC for full time positions at the national and international level that can offer stipends and salaries.
Are there any requirements to joining AIESEC?
Leadership can be developed in anyone, anywhere, meaning we take everyone with a passion for leadership and contributing to making an impact on our world. The only requirement is that all our volunteers need to be between 18–30 years old.
Can I join an international chapter?
At the local level, members should be living in the same region as their chapter. However, as you grow in AIESEC, you have the opportunity to apply for national and international roles in other entities!
What is the selection process like?
After signing up on aiesec.ca you should receive an email from the local chapter you choose with next steps. During recruitment season (typically January, April, and September), you will be prompted to fill out an application depending on the local chapter.
Do I need to pay to be a member?
Not at all! We don't believe leadership should have a price tag so we want to make sure our programs are available and accessible to as many young individuals as possible.
What is the time commitment like?
This depends on the role and time of the term, but we aim to keep our members working 5–10h per week so they can balance with school, work, or any other personal commitments.
